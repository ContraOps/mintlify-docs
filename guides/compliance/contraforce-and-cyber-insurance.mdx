## Overview

ContraForce provides the tools and visibility that organizations need to meet the growing security requirements of cyber insurance providers. By leveraging ContraForce's platform, organizations can demonstrate a strong security posture, streamline policy applications, and maintain ongoing compliance with insurer expectations.

## How ContraForce Supports Cyber Insurance Readiness

### Security Posture Documentation

ContraForce's incident management and reporting capabilities provide the documentation that insurers look for when evaluating an organization's security maturity:

- **Incident tracking and response history** — Demonstrate a structured approach to threat detection and response
- **Gamebook-driven workflows** — Show that your organization follows repeatable, documented response procedures
- **Entity insights and risk visibility** — Provide evidence of continuous monitoring across your environment

### Meeting Common Insurer Requirements

Many cyber insurance providers require policyholders to have specific controls in place. ContraForce helps address common requirements including:

- **24/7 monitoring and detection** — ContraForce's platform provides continuous visibility across connected security tools
- **Incident response planning** — Gamebooks serve as actionable, documented incident response plans
- **Multi-factor authentication oversight** — Monitor and enforce MFA policies through integrated identity management
- **Endpoint protection** — Centralized endpoint visibility through the ContraForce Endpoint page
- **Vulnerability management** — Track and remediate security findings through the ContraForce workbench

### Reporting for Insurance Applications and Renewals

ContraForce can help organizations prepare for insurance applications and renewals by providing:

- Historical incident data and response metrics
- Security tool deployment and coverage reports
- Evidence of ongoing security monitoring and improvement
