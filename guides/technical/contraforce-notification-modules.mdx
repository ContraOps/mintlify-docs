## Overview

ContraForce provides notifications for Microsoft Sentinel incidents. For further documentation on product capabilities for individual XDR and SIEM integrations, refer to [this](https://docs.contraforce.com/knowledge/contraforce-capabilities-matrix) document.

## FAQs

- What is the email address ContraForce will send notifications from?
  - noreply@notifications.contraforce.com

- How do I adjust email notification settings.
  - Within [Settings > Notifications](https://portal.contraforce.com/settings/notifications) you can adjust preferences according to incident severity and workspace.

- What is included in an incident email notification?
  - Title
  - Description
  - MITRE Tactics
  - Entities
  - Link to open the incident in ContraForce
